# RE-OS Revenue Model

**Version:** 1.0

---

## 1. Revenue Streams

| Stream | % Year 1 target | Description |
|--------|-----------------|-------------|
| Subscriptions | 85% | Monthly/annual plans |
| AI minute overage | 10% | Usage above plan |
| Enterprise custom | 5% | Custom contracts, white-label |

---

## 2. Unit Economics (Pro Plan)

| Metric | Value |
|--------|-------|
| ARPU | ₹20,000/month |
| Gross margin target | 75% |
| CAC target | < ₹40,000 |
| LTV (24 mo, 3% churn) | ~₹4,00,000 |
| LTV:CAC | >3:1 |

---

## 3. Pricing Philosophy

- **Value metric:** Properties + seats (aligns with agency growth)  
- **AI as premium:** Drives Pro → Enterprise  
- **No free tier:** Trial only (14 days) to filter serious buyers  

---

## 4. Revenue by Customer Lifecycle

| Stage | Revenue action |
|-------|----------------|
| Trial | ₹0; full Pro features |
| Convert | First subscription charge |
| Expand | Seat/property limits → upgrade |
| Retain | Annual prepay discount 17% |
| Churn | Downgrade to export; win-back email |

---

## 5. Projections (Conservative)

| Month | Paid orgs | MRR (INR) |
|-------|-----------|-----------|
| 6 | 50 | ₹10L |
| 12 | 200 | ₹40L |
| 24 | 800 | ₹1.6Cr |

Assumes 70% Pro, 25% Basic, 5% Enterprise.

---

## 6. CEO Guardrails

- Discounts >20% require annual prepay  
- Enterprise deals minimum ₹1L/month  
- Feature bloat that doesn't affect conversion → reject
