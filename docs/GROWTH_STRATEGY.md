# RE-OS Growth Strategy

**Version:** 1.0

---

## 1. Positioning

**Category:** Vertical CRM + inventory + AI for Indian real estate agencies  
**Tagline:** "The operating system for real estate sales teams"  
**Wedge:** Pipeline discipline + public SEO listings + AI qualification in one product  

---

## 2. ICP (Ideal Customer Profile)

| Attribute | Target |
|-----------|--------|
| Size | 5–50 agents |
| Geography | Tier 1–2 cities (Ahmedabad, Pune, Bangalore first) |
| Pain | WhatsApp lead chaos, no follow-up SLA |
| Budget | ₹5k–20k/month SaaS acceptable |

---

## 3. Go-To-Market Phases

### Phase A — Design Partners (Month 1–3)

- 10 agencies free Pro in exchange for weekly feedback  
- Success metric: 3 agencies close ≥1 extra deal attributed to RE-OS  

### Phase B — Paid Launch (Month 4–6)

- Self-serve signup with 14-day trial  
- Inside sales for Pro upgrades  
- Content: "How Ahmedabad agencies convert leads" case studies  

### Phase C — Scale (Month 7–12)

- Channel partners (RERA consultants, CRM migrators)  
- Programmatic SEO flywheel per tenant  
- AI minutes as expansion revenue  

---

## 4. Acquisition Channels

| Channel | Tactic | CAC target |
|---------|--------|------------|
| SEO | Tenant property pages + RE-OS marketing site | Low |
| LinkedIn | Founder-led content for brokers | Medium |
| Partnerships | Co-marketing with builders | Medium |
| Outbound | 50 agencies/month in one city | High — test only |
| Referral | 1 month free per referred paid org | Low |

---

## 5. Activation Metrics

| Step | Definition | Target |
|------|------------|--------|
| Signup | Org created | — |
| Activated | ≥5 properties + ≥2 employees + 1 inquiry | 60% in 7 days |
| Habit | 10+ inquiry updates/week | 40% of activated |

---

## 6. Retention Plays

- Weekly email digest to org_owner (pipeline summary)  
- Follow-up SLA alerts (reduce churn from "didn't use")  
- Usage-based nudges when under plan limits  

---

## 7. Expansion Revenue

| Lever | Mechanism |
|-------|-----------|
| Seat expansion | More employees |
| Tier upgrade | More properties + AI minutes |
| Add-ons | WhatsApp BSP, custom domain, extra AI pack |

---

## 8. What We Will Not Do (Year 1)

- Generic CRM for non-real-estate  
- Free forever tier (devalues product)  
- US market launch
